Why It Matters

The algorithm may be working against you

Research from KIPPRA, the Kenya FinAccess Survey 2021/2024, and international studies on African fintech have identified systematic biases in how digital credit algorithms score borrowers.

A rural borrower may be scored 8 points lower simply for their location. Women face a documented penalty despite equal repayment behaviour. Your phone model is being used as an income proxy.

This tool — built as part of a JKUAT Data Science thesis — surfaces these penalties and shows you what your score should be without them. Powered by guidelines from the CBK and the Kenya National Bureau of Statistics.
