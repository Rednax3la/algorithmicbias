The Problem

The algorithm may be working against you

Digital lenders use mobile data, GPS location, and your phone model as credit proxies. Research shows these signals encode systematic disadvantages that have nothing to do with your ability to repay.

A rural borrower is penalised 8 points simply for where they live. Women face a documented penalty despite equal repayment behaviour. A feature phone costs you 7 points regardless of your income.

CreditLens surfaces these penalties individually, shows you what your score should fairly be, and cites the research behind each finding.
